# Governance

## Overview

StangaNet is a personal organization maintained by a single owner.
All decisions — technical, directional, and operational — are made by the maintainer.

## Roles

| Role           | Who              | Responsibilities                             |
| -------------- | ---------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| **Maintainer** | @StangaNet       | All decisions, releases, and code ownership  |

There are no external maintainers, committers, or governance committees.

## Decision Making

All changes are decided unilaterally by the maintainer. There is no voting or formal review process.

Feedback from the community is welcome via Issues and Discussions, but it does not imply any commitment to act on it.

## Contributions

External contributions (pull requests) are **not accepted**.
Repositories are published for reference and learning purposes only.

See [CONTRIBUTING.md](./CONTRIBUTING.md) for details.

## Licensing
